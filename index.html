Playgrounds in Varberg

A presentation of the many exciting public playgrounds in and around Varberg.

Play around here to find out more. You might get tempted to visit some of them.

Trönningenäs, Travvägen

Trönningenäs has an exciting playground suitable for all ages. If you bring food, you can spend the entire day here.

Learn more

Barnens badstrand

In the city centre of Varberg there is a really nice beach. This is in particular suitable children's families, but also for people with some disabilities. There are many facilities close at hand as well.

Learn more

Kyrkbacken

In Varberg city centre you find this cosy playground for the young children. Here you can spend several hours if you bring some picknick.
