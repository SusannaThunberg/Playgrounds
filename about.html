Public Playgrounds in Varberg

- nursed by the Harbour and Road Administration

The Harbour and Road Administration is responsible for traffic systems and public environments. The administration develops, plans, operates, builds, and manages the activities within the Harbour and Road Comittee's area of ​​responsibility. one of these responsibilities are the playgrounds in and around Varberg.

Children and young people need inspiring environments to play and stay in. One of the assignments is to create opportunities for different kinds of play.
